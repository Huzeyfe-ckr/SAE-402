How does it work?

Game Objective

Shoot arrows to hit flying targets that navigate through your environment. The more precise you are, the more points you earn!

  • Immersive Archery - Use your hands to draw and shoot the bow
  • Dynamic Targets - Birds, Bee and Bat fly everywhere in the room
  • Scoring System - Bee (3x), Bird (2x), Bat (1x)
  • 1 Minute to achieve the best score possible
  • Real Compatibility - Real walls limit your playfield
